How much water do I use in week?

This calculator takes in a couple of simple facts from your everyday life, and estimates how much water you use in gallons, and then in litres. Finally, it shows how much water you use in comparison to an average citizen on the continent of Africa in a day. Hopefully, it becomes clear how much water you use and helps you appreciate clean water more!
